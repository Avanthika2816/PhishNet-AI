The AI analyzes emails for these phishing indicators:

🔴 Urgency Tactics       - Time pressure, deadlines, \"act now\"
🔴 Data Requests         - Passwords, SSN, credit cards
🔴 Suspicious Links      - Misspelled URLs, non-HTTPS
🔴 Impersonation         - Fake banks, government, companies
🔴 Threats               - Legal action, account closure
🔴 Grammar Issues        - Spelling errors, poor formatting
🔴 Too Good to Be True   - Lottery wins, free money
🔴 Generic Greetings     - \"Dear Customer\" instead of name
🔴 Fear Tactics          - Security breaches, account hacks
🔴 Sender Mismatch       - Email doesn't match claimed sender
